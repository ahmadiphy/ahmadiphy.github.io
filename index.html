About


My name is Abolfazl Ahmadi-Rahmat and my field of study is Statistical Physics. I hold B.Sc. degree in Solid State Physics and I have an M.Sc. in Solid State Physics (Statistical Mechanics and Complex Dynamical Systems) from Shiraz University, Shiraz, Iran. My M.Sc. thesis, supervised by Dr. Afshin Montakhab, was on the criticality and phase transition in the Hierarchical Modular Brain Networks.
